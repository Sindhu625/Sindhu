* What is a Wireframe:

A wireframe is a simple blueprint or layout sketch of a website or app.

* Purpose:

1.Structure of the page

2.Placement of buttons, menus, images

3.How the screens will look

4.User flow (how users move from one page to another)

* Characteristics:

1.No colors

2.No stylish fonts

3.No detailed graphics

4.Only boxes and lines

* Example:

\[Header]

\[Menu]

\[Image]

\[Text]

\[Button]

\[Footer]



* What is Figma:

Figma is an online tool used to design and prototype websites, mobile apps, and UI/UX layouts.

What you can do with Figma:

1.Create wireframes

2.Create UI designs

3.Create app layouts

4.Create prototypes (clickable mockups)

5.Collaborate with teammates in real-time

* Why Figma is popular:

1.Free for beginners

2.Works in the browser (no download required)

3.Easy drag-and-drop design

4.Real-time collaboration like Google Docs
